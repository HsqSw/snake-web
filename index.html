How to play

  • Eat food to grow and score points.
  • Avoid walls and your own body.
  • Each food speeds up the game down to 60ms.

Controls

  • Move with Arrow Keys or WASD.
  • Press R during the game to restart instantly.
  • Use the speed slider to set the base pace before or during play.
